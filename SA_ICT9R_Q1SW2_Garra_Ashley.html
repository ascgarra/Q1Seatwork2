Characters In Noli Me Tangere

Maria Clara Maria Clara de los Santos, also known as Maria Clara, was Crisostomo Ibarra's fiancee and Capitan Tiago's daughter. Maria Clara faced various problems to their marriage proposal once Ibarra returned to San Diego
Crisostomo Ibarra Ibarra was a young man who was sincere and idealistic. He desired to develop the country, influenced by his European education; as part of this, he believed in the potential of education to implement reforms and worked to construct a school in San Diego to that aim.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso was the real father of Maria Clara and the previous town curate of San Diego. The man tried to keep his daughter's marriage to Crisostomo Ibarra apart for the sake of Maria Clara.
Teniente Guevarra Miguel Guevarra, also known as Teniente Guevarra was an elderly Guardia Civil lieutenant and personal friend of Don Rafael Ibarra. With a strong respect for the guy, he later worked to safeguard Don Rafael's son Crisostomo after he returned from Europe.
Capitan Tiago Capitan Tiago was a powerful businessman in San Diego and Maria Clara's father. Capitan Tiago strained to obey the friars' will when he married his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra.
